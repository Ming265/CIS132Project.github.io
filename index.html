Echoes of the Shattered Veil

Xingye - The Heavenly Demon

Born to scavengers in a fragile shack, Xingye’s world shattered when the Murim League’s purge claimed his parents—Lira’s qi fading under a spear, Toren’s axe silenced by swords. Varkis of the Heavenly Demon Cult pulled him from the ashes, naming him “Crimson Veil.” At Level 72, his Mogang blazes with selective justice, but executing a coerced traitor cracks his resolve, qi scorching the ground in guilt.
At the Veilspire, Xingye’s Shadow Strike tore through a qi demon, his crimson tendrils clashing with chaos incarnate. When the Veilweaver conjured his father’s accusing shade, he channeled qi into Jake’s strike, proving justice over weakness in a storm of blood and light.

Jake - The Survivor

Jake was a dropout slinging snacks when the Shattering unleashed monsters, the System’s snarky voice—“Survive? Cute. Try harder”—his only guide. At Level 30, he leads Mia, Raj, and Elena, driven by a qi relic echoing his lost sister’s pendant, her voice haunting his dreams through mist and ruin.
Facing the Veilweaver, Jake’s Power Strike met his sister’s pleading illusion—“Abandon it, live.” Grit defied her, and with Xingye’s qi glowing green-red, he crushed the titan’s core, bat dripping with chaos’s end in a shattered city’s dawn.

Lin Chen - The Gu Wanderer

Lin Chen, Rank 3, survived his village’s ruin, wielding the Ash Gu after burying the Goodwill Gu—its light dimmed by a healed villager’s betrayal to bandits. Leading orphans, little Xiu’s whispers to her Gu shell fuel his pragmatism, the System taunting: “Potential wasted.”
At the Veilspire, Lin Chen’s dust blinded a Gu swarm, his fused Ash-Goodwill shield flaring as Xiu whispered “Thank you.” Poisoned but unbowed, he guided Jake’s strike, Rank 5 earned in a miasmic stand against chaos’s lie.
